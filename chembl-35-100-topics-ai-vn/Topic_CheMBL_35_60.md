*   **L·ªói SQL: `ERROR: operator does not exist: numeric ~ unknown, LINE 12: AND act.standard_value ~ '^[0-9\.]+$'`**

    L·ªói n√†y x·∫£y ra do b·∫°n ƒëang c·ªë g·∫Øng s·ª≠ d·ª•ng to√°n t·ª≠ `~` (regular expression match) tr√™n m·ªôt c·ªôt ki·ªÉu s·ªë (numeric). ƒê·ªÉ kh·∫Øc ph·ª•c, b·∫°n c·∫ßn chuy·ªÉn ƒë·ªïi c·ªôt `standard_value` sang ki·ªÉu text tr∆∞·ªõc khi so s√°nh v·ªõi regular expression.

    ```sql
    SELECT *
    FROM activities act
    WHERE act.standard_type = 'IC50'

    ```
