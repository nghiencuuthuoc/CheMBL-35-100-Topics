3.1. V√≠ d·ª• SQL (SQL Examples)

```sql -- V√≠ d·ª• 1: L·∫•y 100 h·ª£p ch·∫•t c√≥ ho·∫°t t√≠nh ·ª©c ch·∫ø enzyme EGFR (EGFR inhibition activity) -- Example 1: Get 100 compounds with EGFR inhibition activity SELECT DISTINCT molregno, act.standard_value, act.standard_units, compounds.canonical_smiles FROM activities act JOIN target_dictionary td ON act.tid = td.tid JOIN assays ass ON act.assay_id = ass.assay_id JOIN molecule_dictionary md ON act.molregno = md.molregno JOIN compound_structures compounds ON md.molregno = compounds.molregno WHERE td.target_name LIKE '%EGFR%' AND ass.assay_type = 'B' -- 'B' for binding assays AND act.standard_type = 'IC50' AND act.standard_units = 'nM' AND act.standard_value IS NOT NULL AND act.standard_value < 100 -- IC50 < 100 nM (potent compounds) AND act.standard_value ~ '^[0-9.]+$' -- Gi√° tr·ªã standard_value ch·ªâ ch·ª©a s·ªë v√† d·∫•u ch·∫•m LIMIT 100;
